Breakfast Cheese omellete
  • 3 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter

1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add salt and pepper, and beat lightly.

2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

3. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and let them cook, stirring occasionally, until they're set but still moist.

4. Serve hot.
Lunch Alferodo Pasta
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives
  • 2 oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

1. In a large bowl, combine cucumber, tomato, onion, olives, and feta cheese.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.

3. Pour dressing over salad and toss gently to combine.

4. Serve chilled.
Dinner Spaghetti Carbonara
  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 4 slices bacon, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.

2. In a skillet, cook bacon until crispy. Add garlic and cook for another minute.

3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.

4. Drain spaghetti and immediately add to skillet with bacon. Remove from heat.

5. Pour egg mixture over hot spaghetti and toss quickly to coat.

6. Serve immediately.
Dessert Chocolate Cake
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

2. In a large bowl, sift together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.

4. Stir in boiling water. The batter will be thin.

5. Pour batter evenly into prepared pans.

6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

7. Allow cakes to cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and transfer to wire racks to cool completely.

8. Frost with your favorite frosting and enjoy!
