Pomeranian Dog
  • Pomeranians are descendants of larger sled and working dogs from the Arctic regions.
  • They are named after the Pomerania region, which is now part of Poland and Germany.
  • Despite their small size, Pomeranians have a bold, intelligent, and lively personality.
  • Pomeranians have a double coat of fur that's dense and plush, coming in various colors.
  • Their thick double coat sheds profusely, requiring regular grooming.
